## What is this Brand & Technology Upgrade?

In May 2024, the globally renowned creative assets and design platform **Freepik** officially announced the acquisition of **Magnific AI**, the industry-leading tool in the field of AI image super-resolution and hallucination-guided enhancement. 

With this integration, some of Freepik's built-in AI enhancement modules and workflows are migrating and unifying under the more professional, standalone Magnific platform. Whether you are a photographer, concept artist seeking ultimate details, or a designer requiring high-definition assets, this transition represents a significant upgrade in creative potential.

## Notes for Community Users During Migration

* **Accounts & Login:** Although Magnific is owned by Freepik, its billing and account systems currently remain independent from Freepik's main site. You will need to register a dedicated account on the official Magnific platform.
* **Feature Differences:** Magnific offers fine-grained controls (HDR, Creativity, Resemblance, etc.). While this is much more professional than the basic upscaling features previously built into Freepik, it has a steeper learning curve.
* **Pricing Adjustments:** The professional positioning of Magnific means a higher price threshold. It is tailored for commercial projects with ultimate image quality demands.
